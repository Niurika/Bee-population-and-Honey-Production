American Bee Colony Dashboard

Using data provided by the USDA, our team built three visualizations to show how bee farming in the US has changed over the past two decades.

  • Chart 1 shows how the selling price of honey per lb has fluctuated over the course of the study. It is filtered by state and can be changed using the dropdown at the top left corner of the graph.
  • Chart 2 shows the production volume of honey in lbs by year. It is also filtered by state and will update when the dropdown is manipulated.
  • Chart 3 (map) shows the amount of bee colonies in each state for the most recent year in the study (2021). The name of the state and colony count will show in the top right corner of the map when the mouse is positioned inside a state's border.
